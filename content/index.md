> Welcome to your published vault. This page is rendered by [Quartz v4](https://quartz.jzhao.xyz/) from `content/index.md`.

This is the landing page of the template. Replace it with your own notes once you copy your Obsidian vault into the `content/` directory.

## Getting started

1. Replace this file (`content/index.md`) with the entry note of your own vault.
2. Edit `quartz.config.ts` and set `pageTitle`, `baseUrl`, and `locale`.
3. Update `quartz.layout.ts` footer links.
4. Commit, push, and let your platform rebuild the site.

See [[example]] for a tour of Obsidian-flavored markdown features that Quartz supports.
